## Why this course

The best engineers of this decade don't memorize YAML configs. They understand the *concepts* deeply — containerisation, orchestration, pipeline design, observability — and let an AI (Claude, Gemini, Deepseek) generate the manifests. This course is built for that workflow.

Every day you'll:

1. Read the concept notes (free, here on GitHub).
2. Watch the embedded YouTube Shorts for the ideas.
3. On [vibelearner.com](https://vibelearner.com/tracks/devops), open a Pro AI tutor scoped to the lesson, take a quick quiz, and — by Day 10 — run a mock interview for the whole domain.

No copy-pasting tutorials. No Stack Overflow surfing. You learn the *why* and prompt AI for the *how*.
